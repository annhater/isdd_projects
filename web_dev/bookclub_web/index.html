Chill Book Club

Hello!

We are chill book club. It's chill here.

You just grab a book that we choose (almost) every month, sit back, relax, forget about the noise around you, about work, about everything that stresses you.

When you're finished (or not), you can join our monthly discussions, mostly online, so it's more convenient. We are English speaking-club, but language barriers here are bent like it's a sport ;)

We're not specific to any genre, on the contrary, we want to diversify our bookshelves, by reading from different countries, in translation or in original (if you can!), from diverse authors, both classics and contemporary. Although, we do have a big love for modern literature by women... so there will be a lot of that.

So, let our journey begin!
